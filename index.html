Curatorial Statement

Welcome to COMMODIFY, A Virtual Exhibition.

For my Virtual Exhibition, I wanted to explore the relationship between art, consumption and commodification. Throughout the semester, I found myself responding most to the artworks in the lecture whose meaning had changed over the years due to the work itself or the artist’s image being COMMODIFIED.

What does it mean to make art in a profit-driven society? What does it mean to have not just the art, but the artist’s image, commodified? What role have you played in that commodification? Some of the works here feature a button that says COMMODIFY, which when pressed, will show an example of that work’s commodification.
